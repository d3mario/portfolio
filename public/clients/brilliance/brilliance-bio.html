Bril-liance (noun): Exceptional intelligence, ability, skill or talent.
It's easy to see why the name fits the man. From the Detroit suburbs, Brilliance is an amazing talent who crosses all musical styles to create something new, fresh, and exciting. His unique style has been influenced by a diverse list of musicians including Nas, Tupac, Eminem, Hendrix, Clapton and Sublime.


Creativity has always been Brilliance's strongest asset. A graphic designer, producer, writer and emcee—he has what it takes to become a superstar household name. He is a hard-working artist who believes that the road to the top is paved with persistence, fortitude, and a positive attitude.

Brilliance believes in himself so much that he hit the streets of Detroit and showed the city what a strong self-promoter he is. As an un-signed artist, Brilliance has managed to have over a dozen records spinning throughout every major club in Detroit. His songs have been featured in the top mix shows on Detroit radio. Recently, Brilliance had a single in rotation on the #1 top 40 station in Detroit: 95.5. Brilliance's songs online have had hundreds of thousands of hits, and now he is working with top producers in the hip hop community.


The Brilliance songbook is composed of heavy instrumentation and his songs are inspired by funk, rock, rap, dance, jazz, and Motown elements. This alone separates Brilliance from the rest of the music industry today. His wide range and unusual sound have caused a major buzz that extends from New York to the West Coast. Get ready to go through a hip hop, pop rock journey that will blow your mind. It's time for the world to meet- BRILLIANCE!
